Glossary of Terms

Zero-Day:
A vulnerability that is unknown to those who should be interested in its mitigation.
Ransomware:
Malware that encrypts files and demands payment for the decryption key.
MFA:
Multi-Factor Authentication - requiring more than just a password to log in.

Developing Research Skills

Cybersecurity is an ever-evolving field. Here is how to stay ahead:

  • Source Verification: Always cross-reference security alerts across multiple trusted sources.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Follow security researchers on platforms like Mastodon, LinkedIn, or specialized forums.
  • Hands-on Labs: Research is best paired with practice. Use platforms like Hack The Box or TryHackMe.
  • Documentation: Keep a record of your findings and the steps taken to verify them.
